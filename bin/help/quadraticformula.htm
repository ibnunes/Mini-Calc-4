4.2. Quadratic formula


This section allows you to solve any quadratic equation using the quadratic formula:



You just have to insert the parameters a, b and c and Mini Calc will compute everything about it: discriminant, roots and vertex.


When you open the quadratic formula, you'll be shown this:



Introduce the parameters. In the end, you'll be given the resulting information:



As you can see, Mini Calc is able to calculate the imaginary roots if the discriminant is negative. One should note that Mini Calc cannot compute imaginary numbers - this is a very specific feature of the quadratic formula not available anywhere else in the program.


The resulting equation is stored in the variable quadeq.
