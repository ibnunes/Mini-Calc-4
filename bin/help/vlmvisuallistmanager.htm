5.1. VLM - Visual List Manager


The VLM is an interactive system that allows you to completely manipulate the lists visually, knowing exactly what's happening when it's happening.


It is a panel of navigable lists and gives you a great variety of shortcuts to manipulate individually the lists and its values.
