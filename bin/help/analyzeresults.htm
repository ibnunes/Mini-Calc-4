6.2. Analyze results


Given the fact the process is pseudo-aleatory, the Law of the Big Numbers should work perfectly for a high number of tries. In this case, we've made 1000 randomizations.


RanGen shows the list of objects randomized and the number of times each one was "chosen" by the randomization process, followed by the percentage.



You can see the graphic from the menu in the bottom of the screen. You can save the image then by pressing "S" or just close the graph by pressing Enter.
