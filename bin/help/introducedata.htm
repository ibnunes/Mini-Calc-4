6.1. Introduce data


When you enter the RandGen, it'll ask you for a list to randomize from. To introduce this list, you must write its object separated by comma. Note that the spaces are ignored. After that, RandGen asks for a number of tries: this is the number of times RandGen will randomize the objects, testing the Law of the Big Numbers. The maximum number of tries is 400 million (it should take about a minute and half to randomize).



It'll be displayed a message "Running..." while the objects are being randomized. In the end, the message "[done]" is shown and you can proceed to the results by pressing Enter.
