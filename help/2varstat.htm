5.2.2. 2-Var Stat


You'll have the screen divided in two. On the left side, you'll choose two lists available from the VLM. You cannot choose the same list on both times.

On the right side you'll have the results of the analysis. Those results are all saved in the respective variables. It will be created four new lists with the name of th Xi list followed by their contents description.

You can access the bar graph using the menu shown in the bottom of the screen and save it in a BMP image file by pressing "S".
