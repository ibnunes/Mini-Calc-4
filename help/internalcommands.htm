8.3. Internal commands


Mini Calc offers less Internal Commands than in its previous generations. This is due to the fact variables were now introduced alongside with the new calculator.


The commands are the following:

  • cancel - cancels the current operation;
  • exit - exits the application;
  • help - is ignored - it is a pre-implementation for future versions.


You can't use these commands everywhere. There are some systems the Internal Commands cannot be embedded.
